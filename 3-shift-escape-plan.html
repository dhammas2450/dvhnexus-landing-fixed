The 3-Shift Escape Plan

Do one focused action per shift. Each action is phone-first and takes 15-30 minutes.

🎯 SHIFT 1 — Tool Setup (15 minutes)

Goal: Get your AI team ready

What you're building:
  • Content Writer → ChatGPT / Kimi (writes sales pages, emails, social posts)
  • Graphic Designer → Ideogram AI (creates logos, images)
  • Video Editor → AI clipping + CapCut (repurpose content)
  • Website/Links → Link-in-bio / Vercel (hosts your presence)
Your 15-Minute Action:
  1. Install ChatGPT app on your phone
  2. Install CapCut (free video editor)
  3. Create a Link-in-bio (Linktree or Koji - free)
  4. Save this prompt: "Write a 30-45 second TikTok script to promote a free checklist about shift-friendly side hustles"

💰 SHIFT 2 — First Sale (15-30 minutes)

Goal: Get paid while you learn

The Method: Affiliate Marketing
  • Promote other people's products
  • Earn commission (30-50% per sale)
  • No inventory, no customer service, no risk
  • Do it from your phone on breaks
Your 30-Minute Action:
  1. Pick a tool you use and believe in
  2. Search "[tool name] affiliate program"
  3. Sign up and get your affiliate link
  4. Use ChatGPT to generate 3 short video scripts promoting it
  5. Post 1 video with your Link-in-bio link
  6. DM 10 likely buyers with a simple message

My First Dollar: I promoted a tool I was already using. Someone clicked my link. I made $47. That was proof the system worked.

🚀 SHIFT 3 — Scale & Systemize (Ongoing)

Goal: Replace your Walmart income

Month 1: Build the Machine
  • Set up AI tools (Week 1)
  • Create your first simple offer (Week 2)
  • Start sharing content daily (Weeks 3-4)
  • Target: First $50-200
Month 2: Scale What Works
  • Double down on content that gets engagement
  • Build your email list
  • Add one upsell or additional offer
  • Target: $500-1000/month
Month 3: Replace the Paycheck
  • Systematize with AI automation
  • Add higher-ticket offers
  • Build recurring revenue
  • Target: $1500-3000/month = Freedom
